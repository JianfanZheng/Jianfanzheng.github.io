Researches

My current works focus on:

Variations of the Pacific ocean during orbital and millennial climate changes.

  • Quantifying the lithogenic dust flux input to the ocean by U-Th isotopes.
  • Reconstruction of paleo ocean circulation by Pa/Th.
  • Exploring the impact of iron-fertilization on marine productivity.

The burial mechanisms of elements at the seawater-sediment interface.

  • Looking into the budgets and burial mechanisms of various elements (such as: Si, Fe, Mn, Co, Ni, Cu, Zn) to the seafloor.
  • Investigating the kinetics of solute transportation at the seafloor through constraints from U-Th isotopes.
